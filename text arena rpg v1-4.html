Welcome to the Text Arena Tutorial!

The Text Arena is a small turn based game. The objective is to defeat the enemy before they kill you!

When you enter the arena an opponent will be selected for you. Since this is a turn based game a coin will be tossed to determine who has the first turn

If it is your turn you have as much time as you would like to make a decision. Currently all choices are up and running except for "magic", which will be ready soon.

If it is the enemy's turn the computer will take a moment to make a decision (the computer has the same options as you).

Most importantly, have fun!
